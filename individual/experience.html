Experience

This program gave me a valuable experience as this was my first time participating in a great program. Various challenges and skills that I learned while participating. I also had the opportunity to attend a course created by the Nescafe team to teach us how to make nescafe recipes properly. I feel so grateful to have participated in this great program and it has also given me invaluable learning.
